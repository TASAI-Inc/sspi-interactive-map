This indicator tracks the status of FMSS-compliant national seed policy instruments (seed policy, law, regulations and decrees) and institutional arrangements that encourage private sector/community participation in seed sector development. For a top score, a country should have a seed policy or strategy that recognizes and promotes FMSS, as well as a stand-alone legislative or regulatory instrument on FMSS. For a score of 6 to less than 8, countries should recognize FMSS in policy instruments and promote some of their aspects. In countries scoring 4 to less than 6, FMSS are recognized in policy instruments, but minimal actual support is provided to these systems. For a score of 2 to less than 4, FMSS are recognized in policy instruments and minimal support is provided. A score of 0 to less than 2 indicates that the policy instruments that recognize FMSS are in draft form and FMSS receive minimal support. A country whose seed policy instruments do not recognize FMSS scores 0.
